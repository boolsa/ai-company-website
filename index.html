Many small AI tools. One compounding impact.

Microve means micro + improvement. The name is the entire philosophy. We don't build one app that claims to do everything. We build many focused AI tools — each one solves a specific problem in your business, each one delivers a measurable result.

Small improvements compound. A tool that cuts invoice processing from 40 minutes to 4. Another that flags at-risk accounts before your team notices. Another that routes inbound requests without human sorting. None of them is the holy grail. All of them together change how your business operates.

Why Microve exists

Most AI is an encyclopedia. Your business needs tools that do actual work.

You already know about ChatGPT and large language models. You can ask them questions and get answers. That's useful for gaining knowledge — but it doesn't process your invoices, sort your leads, or flag problems in your pipeline. The gap between knowing something and doing something is where most businesses stall with AI. We close that gap. We take the specific capabilities of AI and build them into tools that perform real tasks inside your actual workflow.

We don't sell you a platform and expect you to become an AI expert. We provide a service. You describe your business problems. We design, build, and implement the AI tools that solve them.

We are AI experts — not because of a credential, but because we build and use these tools ourselves. We built AI tools for our own operations before we ever offered this as a service. We understand what works, what breaks, and what this fast-changing industry actually makes possible today — not in theory, but in practice. That experience is the foundation of every tool we build for our clients.

The process

From your problem to a working tool.

Step 1

Listen

We start with your business, not our technology. You tell us where time is wasted, where errors happen, where things are manual that AI should handle.

Step 2

Propose

We map AI capabilities to your problems and present clear solutions. What each tool will do, what it will affect, and what result you should expect.

Step 3

Design

We design and build each AI tool to fit your systems and your workflow. Not a generic product — a purpose-built AI tool that does one job well inside your operation.

Step 4

Implement

We deploy the tool into your existing workflow and measure what it does. Time saved, errors reduced, tasks handled by AI instead of people. If the numbers don't move, the tool isn't done.
