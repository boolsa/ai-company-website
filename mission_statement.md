# Microve.ai — Mission, Philosophy & Approach

## Our Core Belief

**We fit tools to your problems. We never fit your problems into tools.**

Most technology companies sell you a product, then convince you to change how you work to use it. We do the opposite. We look at how you actually work, then build tools that fit into your existing workflow.

---

## What "Micro-Automation" Means

**A micro-automation handles ONE specific workflow bottleneck—not your entire operation.**

- One tool processes your expense receipts
- One tool qualifies your sales leads  
- One tool compiles your weekly reports

We solve one problem completely before moving to the next. Small wins compound. Big bangs blow up.

**Example:** We built a tool for a logistics company that reads incoming shipment emails and automatically creates entries in their Monday.com board—saving them 3 hours every morning.

**Why this approach works:**
- You see results in weeks, not months
- Your team adopts one change at a time
- If something breaks, only one piece breaks
- Each automation pays for itself before the next one starts

## How We Work: Listen First

**Step 1: You describe the problem**
Tell us what's wasting time, what's frustrating your team, where work gets stuck. No jargon needed. Example problems: "We spend 10 hours a week sorting support tickets" or "I manually copy-paste data between three systems."

**Step 2: We question the brief**
We evaluate whether the problem you described is the actual problem. Often it's not. We'll tell you. Sometimes the real problem is three steps upstream from where you think it is.

**Step 3: We propose a solution**
We explain what we think you actually need, why, and what it will cost. You decide if it makes sense. We identify which AI functions can solve your problem—document reading, data extraction, classification—and present specific solutions.

**Step 4: We build and hand it over**
You get a working tool, documentation, and training. We build the automation and integrate it into your existing software—Slack, Salesforce, your email, wherever you work. Then you own it.

## What We Won't Do

**We say no to about 80% of requests.** Here are some things we refuse:

- **Chatbots for basic FAQ pages.** Use Intercom or Zendesk. You don't need custom AI.
- **Full "digital transformation" projects.** We solve specific problems, not vague mandates.
- **Projects that require you to change your entire workflow.** If the solution is harder than the problem, it's not a solution.
- **Anything we can't deliver in under a month.** Big projects go wrong. We keep them small.

**If a $20 Zapier integration fixes it, we'll tell you.** We don't sell you expensive solutions you don't need.
