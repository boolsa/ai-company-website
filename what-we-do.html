The Landscape

How micro-tools differ from the alternatives

When a workflow breaks, businesses typically choose one of three paths.

Off-the-shelf AI tools

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini

  • Great for content & brainstorming
  • Instant answers to questions
  • Requires manual prompting
  • No workflow integration
  • Copy-paste overhead

Hiring someone in-house

Dev hire, ops manager, VA

  • Powerful & extensible
  • Handles complex logic
  • Complex configuration
  • Requires technical expertise
  • Overkill for most SMBs

Custom Micro-Tools

Built for your workflow

  • Zero configuration by you
  • Integrates with your tools
  • Runs automatically in background
  • Surgical fixes for specific steps
  • No technical expertise needed

The difference: Off-the-shelf AI still requires manual operation. Hiring in-house introduces immense overhead. Micro-tools run quietly in the background, designed specifically for your current stack, and deployed in weeks rather than months.
