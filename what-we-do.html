OUR PROCESS

From Problem to Production in Four Steps

Every engagement follows the same disciplined path. You describe the problem. We handle everything else.

01

Listen

Understanding your business before proposing solutions

You tell us what's wrong. We dig in with an operator's lens—evaluating whether your initial read matches the real problem, because we've learned that the first explanation is rarely the full story.

  • • Discovery session — You walk us through the problem in your own terms, no jargon required
  • • Stakeholder interviews — We learn your workflows, constraints, and capabilities
  • • Problem Definition document — Captures exactly what needs to change and why
02

Propose

Translating your problem into a concrete plan

Instead of building exactly what was asked for, we propose what should be built—with reasoning you can evaluate. Once you decide, we implement. No PowerPoint. Just working software.

  • • Data & architecture review — We identify the right A.I. approach for your situation
  • • Clear proposal document — Scope, timeline, outcomes, and cost in plain language
  • • Explicit boundaries — What we're building AND what we deliberately chose not to
03

Design and Build

Engineering a solution tailored to your workflow

This is where the solution takes shape. Our engineers design the A.I. system, build the models, and integrate them into your existing tools and workflows.

  • • End-to-end development — Models, interfaces, and integration as one coordinated effort
  • • Working prototypes — See the solution evolve with regular progress updates
  • • Custom-built tool — Not a generic platform configured with your logo
04

Implement and Measure

Deploying results you can actually see

Delivery does not end at deployment. We roll the solution into your live environment, show your team how to use it, and monitor performance against the success metrics defined in the proposal.

  • • Live deployment — Full integration into your existing tools and workflows
  • • Performance monitoring — We track against the success metrics defined upfront
  • • Clear results report — Time saved, errors reduced, decisions accelerated

YOUR OPTIONS

Three Ways to Get A.I. Into Your Business

Each path has real strengths and real limitations. Here is an honest look at how they compare.

Off the Shelf A.I.

General purpose tools like ChatGPT and similar platforms

  • Fast to start using immediately
  • Low upfront cost
  • Good for general tasks like drafting and research
  • Not built for your specific workflow
  • Output depends on how well your team prompts it
  • No integration with your internal systems or data

Hiring In House

Bringing on a developer or A.I. ops manager

  • Full time resource dedicated to your business
  • Deep internal knowledge over time
  • Direct control over priorities
  • Expensive to recruit and retain specialized talent
  • Single point of failure if that person leaves
  • Long ramp up time before the first tool is operational

Microve

Custom A.I. built around your specific problem

  • Solutions designed for your exact workflow and data
  • Full service from discovery through deployment
  • No hiring or technical management required on your side
  • Integrated directly into the tools your team already uses
  • You own the solution and the results it produces
  • Questions your brief with real business experience, then delivers working software

Off the shelf tools solve general problems. In house hires solve problems over time. We solve your specific problem, then hand you a working tool that keeps solving it without us.
