What we do

We build micro-automations that remove specific repetitive steps from your workflow.

What micro-automation actually means

This is not about "replacing your team with AI." It's about removing friction.

  • We target specific bottlenecks. When a task repeats (moving data, generating updates, routing information), we make it run automatically.
  • Surgical implementation. We don't overhaul your entire company. We fix the 3 steps that take up 40% of your week.
  • Your team keeps control. They just stop doing the copy-paste grunt work and start focusing on execution and judgment.

Why this beats generic AI

General LLMs (like ChatGPT) are great at answering questions. But businesses need actions.

  • It runs in the background. You don't have to open a browser window and type a prompt every time you want work done.
  • It integrates with your tools. We connect directly to your CRM, Slack, Email, and internal databases.
  • It executes on its own. Instead of telling you how to do a task, the automation simply does it for you.
