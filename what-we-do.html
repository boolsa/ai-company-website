The Landscape

How micro-tools differ from the alternatives

When a workflow breaks, businesses typically choose one of three paths.

Off-the-shelf AI tools

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini

  • Great for content & brainstorming
  • Instant answers to questions
  • Requires manual prompting
  • No workflow integration
  • Copy-paste overhead

Hiring someone in-house

Dev hire, ops manager, VA

  • Powerful & extensible
  • Handles complex logic
  • Complex configuration
  • Requires technical expertise
  • Overkill for most SMBs

Custom Micro-Tools

Built for your workflow

  • Zero configuration by you
  • Integrates with your tools
  • Runs automatically in background
  • Surgical fixes for specific steps
  • No technical expertise needed

The difference: Off-the-shelf AI still requires manual operation. Hiring in-house introduces immense overhead. Micro-tools run quietly in the background, designed specifically for your current stack, and deployed in weeks rather than months.

Real Client: Law Firm

A law firm drowning in manual client onboarding—prospect calls, manual CRM entry, document drafting, and follow-up tracking. We built a system that turns prospects into onboarded clients in hours, not days.

1

Phase 1: Discovery

Understanding your bottleneck

1 MAP

Map current workflow

Document the journey from prospect call → consultation → contract → case file creation. Identify every manual handoff and delay.

2 IDENTIFY

Identify automation candidates

Pinpoint repetitive tasks: data entry, document generation, email sequences, calendar scheduling.

3 PRIORITIZE

Prioritize by impact

Focus on the steps eating the most time: contract drafting (2 hrs), document collection (3 hrs), follow-up tracking (1 hr daily).

4 VALIDATE

Validate with your team

Review findings with paralegals and partners. Confirm pain points and get buy-in before building.

2

Phase 2: Design

Planning the solution

5 ARCHITECT

Design the automation architecture

Choose the right mix: Zapier for triggers, custom tool for document generation, AI for data extraction.

6 INTEGRATE

Map integrations

Connect systems: Phone → CRM (Clio) → Document tool → Email → Calendar. Ensure data flows seamlessly.

7 CUSTOMIZE

Build custom tools

Create custom document templates, intake forms, and approval workflows tailored to the firm's practice areas.

8 PLAN

Plan testing & rollout

Design pilot with 5 test clients, define success metrics (time saved, error reduction), plan training schedule.

3

Phase 3: Build

Creating the automation

9 DEVELOP

Develop automation workflows

Build the triggers: New call logged → Auto-create case file → Generate retainer agreement → Schedule follow-up.

10 CODE

Code custom tools

Build custom document generator, intake form with conditional logic, client portal for document upload.

11 TEST

Test with real data

Run 10 test cases end-to-end. Check document accuracy, data field mapping, email deliverability.

12 REFINE

Refine based on feedback

Adjust workflows based on paralegal input. Fix edge cases. Add approval checkpoints where needed.

4

Phase 4: Deploy

Implementation & handoff

13 PILOT

Pilot with select cases

Run live pilot with 5 real clients. Monitor closely. Gather feedback daily. Adjust in real-time.

14 TRAIN

Train your team

Hands-on training for all users. Video recordings, documentation, live Q&A. Ensure everyone's confident.

15 HANDOFF

Handoff & ongoing support

Full system documentation, monitoring dashboard, 30-day support included. You're autonomous but never alone.

Result: Intake time reduced from 2 days to 2 hours

Prospects now move seamlessly from call to onboarded client. Paralegals spend time on legal work, not paperwork. The firm can handle 3x more clients without hiring.

Common Challenge: Sales Teams

Leads come in from multiple sources—website forms, LinkedIn, referrals—but sit in an inbox for hours or days before anyone responds. By then, they've gone cold. We built a system that routes leads instantly and ensures immediate follow-up.

1

Phase 1: Discovery

Understanding your bottleneck

1 MAP

Map lead sources & flow

Document every entry point: website forms, LinkedIn messages, email, phone. Track where leads go and how long they wait.

2 IDENTIFY

Find the delays

Identify bottlenecks: Leads sit in inbox for 6 hours. Manual assignment takes 2 hours. No visibility into follow-up.

3 PRIORITIZE

Prioritize high-value fixes

Focus on speed-to-lead: Auto-response within 5 minutes, intelligent routing, instant CRM entry.

4 VALIDATE

Validate with sales team

Get input from sales reps on routing rules, territory assignments, and follow-up preferences.

2

Phase 2: Design

Planning the solution

5 ARCHITECT

Design intelligent routing

Create rules: Enterprise leads → Senior AE, SMB leads → Junior rep, by territory, by product interest.

6 INTEGRATE

Connect all lead sources

Integrate: Website → CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce) → Slack → Email. One unified pipeline.

7 CUSTOMIZE

Build enrichment tools

Create tools that auto-enrich leads (LinkedIn lookup, company data, scoring) before routing.

8 PLAN

Plan escalation & fallback

Design backup rules: If rep doesn't respond in 15 min → escalate to manager. After hours → assign to on-call.

3

Phase 3: Build

Creating the automation

9 DEVELOP

Build routing workflows

Create automation: Form submitted → Score lead → Route to rep → Send Slack alert → Auto-response email.

10 CODE

Code custom scoring tool

Build lead scoring algorithm based on your criteria: company size, job title, behavior, source quality.

11 TEST

Test routing logic

Test 50 sample leads through the system. Verify routing accuracy, response times, notification delivery.

12 REFINE

Refine based on feedback

Adjust routing rules based on rep feedback. Fix scoring weights. Optimize response templates.

4

Phase 4: Deploy

Implementation & handoff

13 PILOT

Pilot with live leads

Run parallel for 1 week: Old system vs new automation. Compare response times, conversion rates, rep satisfaction.

14 TRAIN

Train sales team

Show reps how to use the new system. Set expectations for response times. Provide mobile app training.

15 HANDOFF

Handoff & optimize

Full system live. Dashboard for managers to monitor response times. Monthly optimization calls. 30-day support.

Result: Every lead contacted within 5 minutes, 24/7

No more leads sitting in inboxes. No more manual assignment. Sales reps get notified instantly with full context. Conversion rates increase 40% just from speed-to-lead alone.
