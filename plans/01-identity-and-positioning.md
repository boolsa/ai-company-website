# Identity and Positioning — Informational Site

## Who We Are (Clear Identity)

**Not:** AI consultants, automation experts, digital transformation specialists
**Actually:** People who build small, working tools for specific problems

**The difference:**
- "AI consultant" = sells advice and strategy decks
- "Automation expert" = implements platforms you don't need  
- "We build small tools" = delivers working code that solves your problem

---

## What We Do (Plain Language)

**Instead of:** "We map AI capabilities to your workflow bottlenecks"
**Say:** "We look at what's wasting your time, then build a tool to automate it"

**Instead of:** "Custom AI solutions for enterprise workflows"
**Say:** "Small tools that handle one specific job, built to fit how you already work"

**Instead of:** "Digital transformation through intelligent automation"
**Say:** "We eliminate tedious work one task at a time"

---

## Our Philosophy (What We Believe)

### 1. Small Beats Big
Big AI projects fail because they try to do everything at once. We solve one problem completely before moving to the next.

### 2. Custom Beats Generic  
Your workflow exists for reasons. We build tools that fit into it, not tools that force you to change.

### 3. Reality Beats Hype
We know what AI can actually do today. We don't promise magic. We deliver working code.

### 4. Listen First
The problem you describe isn't always the real problem. We ask questions before we build.

### 5. You Own It
No platforms. No subscriptions. No vendor lock-in. We build it, we hand it over, you own it.

---

## Who We Help (Specific)

**Good fits:**
- Service businesses with repetitive data work
- Companies using 3+ software tools that don't talk to each other
- Teams spending 5+ hours per week on manual tasks
- Businesses that have tried off-the-shelf software and found it doesn't fit

**Not good fits:**
- Companies wanting "AI transformation" without specific problems
- Businesses that can solve their problem with a $20/month SaaS tool
- Organizations requiring six-month enterprise implementations
- Anyone who wants to replace their workforce with AI

---

## What Makes Us Different (Specifics, Not Claims)

### We Question Briefs
Most developers build exactly what you ask for. We ask if that's what you actually need. Sometimes a $20 Zapier fix is better than custom AI.

### We've Run Businesses
We've had payroll due and broken processes killing margins. We know what operational problems actually cost. That perspective changes how we solve them.

### We Say No
We turn down about 80% of requests. If your problem doesn't need custom automation, we'll tell you. We'd rather be honest than bill hours.

### We Don't Do Platforms
You don't log into our software. The tools we build live in apps you already use. We hand over the code and documentation. You own it completely.

## Tone Guidelines

**DO:**
- Write like you're explaining to a smart friend
- Use specific examples
- Admit limitations and what you won't do
- Show your work
- Be humble about AI's capabilities

**DON'T:**
- Use consultant speak
- Make vague promises
- Hide information to force contact
- Pretend AI is magic
- Claim to be "disruptive" or "innovative"
