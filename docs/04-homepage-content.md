## What Micro-Automation Looks Like (3 Examples)

### Example 1: Expense Receipt Processing
**The Problem:** An accounting firm receives 200+ receipt photos per day via email. Someone manually downloads each one, extracts the amount and date, and enters it into QuickBooks. Takes 3-4 hours daily.

**The Tool:** Software watches the email inbox, reads each receipt image using AI, extracts the relevant data, and enters it directly into QuickBooks. Flags anything unclear for human review.

**Result:** 4 hours/day → 15 minutes/day reviewing exceptions

### Example 2: Sales Lead Qualification
**The Problem:** A B2B services company gets 50+ inquiries per day. Someone has to read each one, determine if it's a good fit, and route it to the right salesperson. Many slip through cracks.

**The Tool:** An AI system reads each inquiry, scores it based on criteria the sales team defined, and routes hot leads to senior reps while sending nurture sequences to others.

**Result:** Zero leads slip through. Sales team focuses on qualified prospects only.

### Example 3: Weekly Client Reports
**The Problem:** A marketing agency spends every Monday morning pulling data from 4 different platforms, copying it into a spreadsheet, formatting it, and creating charts.

**The Tool:** Automated workflow pulls data from all four sources Sunday night, populates the report template, generates charts, and emails the completed report Monday at 6 AM.

**Result:** 6 hours every Monday → 30 minutes reviewing and sending
